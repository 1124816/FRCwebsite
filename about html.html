Welcome to the official website for the STEM School robotics team. Our school's mission is to develop and share a new paradigm for world-class education using technology and as a gateway to cultivate student's inquisitive nature, excerisise innovation, think critically, and collaborate to become leaders who are self sufficent, with the passion of Chattanooga's Renaissance. Through STEM Robotics, students gain a passion to learn, everlasting ability to excersise leadership, and work their minds to come up with innovative and unique ideas.
